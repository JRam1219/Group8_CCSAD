About Us

Our Mission

At the Muntinlupa City Public Library (MCPL), our mission is to provide access to quality educational resources, foster a love for reading, and offer a safe and welcoming environment for the community. We aim to empower individuals through access to knowledge, culture, and information, helping them achieve personal and professional growth.

Our Vision

We envision the Muntinlupa City Public Library as the leading institution in the region, offering diverse programs, services, and a wide variety of materials that inspire lifelong learning. Our goal is to be a central hub of knowledge, creativity, and community engagement.

Our History

The Muntinlupa City Public Library has been serving the residents of Muntinlupa by offering a wide range of services including free access to books, e-books, workshops, and community events. Our library has evolved over the years, integrating modern technology and creating programs that are aligned with the needs of our community.
